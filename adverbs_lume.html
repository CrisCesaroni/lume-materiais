Grammar — Adverbs of manner
How to form them
Tap each rule to see a real example
Rule 1 · most adjectives
+ -ly
careful → carefully
quick → quickly
"She plans her content carefully before posting."
Rule 2 · ends in -e
-e → -ly
simple → simply
immediate → immediately
"He explains things simply so everyone understands."
Rule 3 · ends in -y
-y → -ily
healthy → healthily
easy → easily
"She eats healthily — lots of vegetables and fruit."
Rule 4 · no change
same form
fast → fast
hard → hard
"She works hard every day and runs fast at the gym!"
Rule 5 · irregular — memorise!
good → well  ·  bad → badly
These don't follow any pattern — learn them separately.
"She sings well but dances badly. You can't have everything!"
Position rule
After the verb → "She speaks clearly" · "He works hard"
Before adj/adv → "really hard" · "quite slowly"
